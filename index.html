About this Portfolio

A Journal of Growth

The objective of this website is to present and showcase my progress throughout the different learning cycles of the trimester, highlighting the development of my English skills, digital literacy, research abilities, and ethical awareness related to AI and technology.

It aims to organize my achievements, reflections, and projects in a creative and accessible way while demonstrating my academic growth over time.

Cycle 01

Cycle One

Foundations · Diagnostics · Digital Awareness

Cycle Overview

In this cycle, I will identify my initial level of English proficiency, digital literacy, and ethical awareness related to AI and digital relationships.

I will complete diagnostic activities, reflect on my personal digital habits, and become familiar with the project structure, assessment matrix, digital platforms, and tools that will be used throughout the trimester.

This cycle will help me build the conceptual and language foundation needed to guide my learning during the project.

Cycle 02

Cycle Two

Research · Synthesis · Referencing

Cycle Overview

In this cycle, I will synthesize and organize the articles, film notes, and infographics I have gathered into a structured digital bibliography using online tools such as Annotate.

I will categorize my references into three analytical areas: Human Practices and AI Interaction, Security and Device Protection, and Damage Control regarding data exposure.

I will write summaries for each category using reported speech to combine ideas from different authors and first conditional structures to explain possible consequences of the behaviors identified.

This organized database will become the evidence-based core of my project, which I will later present orally to identify key patterns in how technology reshapes human thought and identity. All this information will be compiled in a cloud-based .doc file that can be revised and shared through a link.

Cycle 03

Cycle Three

IELTS Focus · Four Skills · Exam Strategies

Cycle Overview

In this cycle, I will strengthen my core English language skills through IELTS-oriented tasks.

I will work on the four main sections of the exam: Reading, Writing, Listening, and Speaking, while improving grammar, academic vocabulary, and exam techniques.

Through guided activities using authentic IELTS materials and reflective practice, I will build confidence and become more aware of my current proficiency level.

By the end of the cycle, I will be able to apply effective test-taking strategies and demonstrate linguistic precision in all four skills.

Cycle 04

Cycle Four

Academic Research · Reliable Sources · Reflection

Cycle Overview

In this cycle, I will move from conceptual exploration to structured academic research.

I will locate, evaluate, summarize, and cite reliable academic sources related to AI and human behavior.

I will use AI tools as supports for organization and language refinement, while keeping all evaluative decisions under my own responsibility.

With this information, I will reflect and comment in class about the relevance of my findings for the awareness campaign I will later apply to improve digital usage in my school.

Cycle 05

Cycle Five

Awareness Campaign · Audience · Persuasion

Cycle Overview

In this cycle, I will use my research progress to identify an issue related to electronic devices and AI in order to design an awareness campaign in groups.

I will define a real audience within the school community, establish ethical objectives, and create persuasive messages based on research evidence.

I will focus on language accuracy, tone adaptation, and responsible use of AI and electronic devices while applying conditionals, reported speech, proper collocations, and persuasive expressions to achieve effective communication.

Cycle 06

Cycle Six

Final Production · Feedback · Performance

Cycle Overview

In this cycle, I will produce, refine, and rehearse the final versions of my electronic devices and AI awareness campaign.

I will focus on linguistic accuracy, coherence, visual design quality, and oral performance.

Through continuous peer and teacher feedback, I will improve my materials to ensure they are ethical, research-based, and effective.

I will also continue practicing different parts of the IELTS examination using my support materials as preparation for the final presentation, where I will explain my campaign's effectiveness, share the theoretical framework, present pictures of the campaign process, describe how it was created, and provide a final reflection with appropriate referencing and Creative Commons protection for my authorship.

Cycle Seven

Implementation · Dialogue · Social Impact

Cycle Overview

In this cycle, I will implement and share my electronic devices and AI awareness campaign within the school community.
